Our story

Built on trust, one customer at a time.

Mimipec Enterprise started with a frustration many Nigerians know too well — the worry of not knowing whether the gadget you're buying is genuine, fairly priced, or backed by anyone who'll still pick your call afterwards.

We set out to be the answer to that worry. From our store on Kofo Abayomi Avenue in Apapa, we stock original devices from the brands people trust, sell them at honest prices, and take the time to help each customer choose what's right for them. No pressure, no clones, no stories.

Today, customers across Lagos — and beyond, through nationwide delivery — count on us for phones, accessories, sound and home gadgets. That trust is the thing we're most proud of, and the thing we protect with every sale.

We'd rather lose a sale than sell you the wrong thing. That's how trust is built.

— The Mimipec promise
